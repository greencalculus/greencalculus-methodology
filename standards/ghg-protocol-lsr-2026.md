## Why this standard matters

The Land Sector and Removals Standard (LSR 2026) is the first
GHG Protocol standard to formally address:

- Agricultural land management emissions and removals
- Biological carbon removal (afforestation, reforestation, soil carbon)
- Permanence and reversal risk for land-based carbon credits
- Additionality assessment for removal claims

It was published 30 January 2026 and supersedes earlier land-sector
guidance scattered across GHG Protocol supplementary materials.

---

## How GreenCalculus applies it

### Agricultural calculators
Land management emission factors follow LSR 2026 Annex tables
where available, falling back to IPCC 2006 Guidelines Tier 1
defaults for regions not covered by LSR 2026.

### Carbon removal calculators
GreenCalculus applies the LSR 2026 three-part test for removal claims:

1. **Permanence** — removal must be secured for ≥ 100 years or
   a reversal buffer applied
2. **Additionality** — removal must exceed national/regional baseline
3. **Leakage** — displacement of emissions to other areas assessed

Results from removal calculators are displayed as gross removals.
Netting against Scope 1/2/3 emissions is not performed automatically
— users must apply netting rules per their reporting framework.

# IEA Global Energy Review 2026 — GreenCalculus Alignment

**Publication:** IEA Global Energy Review 2026  
**Published by:** International Energy Agency (IEA)  
**Publisher URL:** https://www.iea.org  
**GreenCalculus stack layer:** Layer 1 — Grid Emission Factor Source

---

## Role in GreenCalculus

The IEA Global Energy Review 2026 is the primary source for
**Scope 2 location-based grid emission factors** for non-UK,
non-US countries in the GreenCalculus dataset.

It provides:
- Annual electricity generation mix by country
- CO2 intensity of electricity generation (g CO2 per kWh)
- Energy demand and supply trends by region

---

## Update cadence

IEA publishes updated energy reviews annually. GreenCalculus
reviews grid factors annually and bumps the dataset version
when any factor changes by more than 2%.

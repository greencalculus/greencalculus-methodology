## How GreenCalculus applies it

Each GreenCalculus Scope 3 calculator is tagged to one of the 15
categories above. Calculator pages display the category number and
name prominently so users can map outputs directly to their
corporate inventory.

### Calculation approaches supported

GreenCalculus calculators implement one or more of the four
GHG Protocol Scope 3 calculation approaches:

- **Spend-based** — expenditure × EEIO emission factor
- **Average-data** — quantity × average emission factor
- **Supplier-specific** — actual supplier data (where available)
- **Hybrid** — combination of the above

The approach used is disclosed on every calculator page.
